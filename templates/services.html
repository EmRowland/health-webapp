What We Do

Child Neurology

Child Neurology is a medical subspecialty that focuses on the diagnosis, treatment, and management of neurological disorders in children, infants, and adolescents

Neuro-oncology

Neuro-oncology is a medical subspecialty that focuses on the diagnosis and treatment of brain and spinal cord tumors.

Neuro-critical care

Neuro-critical care is a specialized field of medicine that focuses on the management of critically ill patients with acute neurological conditions.

Vascular Neurology (stroke)

Vascular neurology, also known as neuro-vascular medicine, is a medical specialty that focuses on the diagnosis and treatment of diseases related to the blood vessels of the brain

Neuro-Muscular disorders

Neuromuscular disorders are a diverse group of medical conditions that affect the neuromuscular system, which includes the nerves controlling voluntary muscles and the muscles themselves

Parkinson's and Movement disorders

Parkinson's disease and movement disorders encompass a diverse group of neurological conditions that primarily affect a person's ability to control and coordinate their movements
